Who We Are

Omar Khattab AlBelbisy Trading Est. is one of the leading institutions in the field of industrial equipment trading in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. Soon after the Kingdom's industrial revolution started, our establishment began working in this field to play an important role in the industrial development of the Kingdom.

Taking into account the requirements of the industrial market for factories, lathes, and equipment for forming and maintenance workshops, our extensive inventory encompasses a diverse selection of metals including brass and copper bars, as well as sheets. We also provide engineering plastics such as PA6 and PTFE imported from Germany. Additionally, we offer a wide array of hand taps, dies, lathe parts, and tools imported from Poland, available in various sizes to meet your specific needs.
